STEPS TO BUY ACEX

Generate Address

Go to My wallet section. Depending on the currency you wish to buy your tokens against, click the deposit button.

Deposit Funds

Scan/Copy the barcode/address and transfer the currency from your personal wallet to it. Wait for the balance to reflect in your account.

Buy Acex

From the dashboard, select the currency you just deposited and enter the number of tokens you wish to buy. If there is sufficient balance, you can click the submit button. The number of tokens would reflect in your dashboard.

Withdraw Acex

To complete this step, approved KYC is mandatory. Since ACEX is an ERC20 contract, you can withdraw it to your ethereum wallets. Withdrawals would be disabled till the crowdsale is over.
